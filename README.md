# BMW Global Sales Analysis (2018–2025) – SQL Project

## Project Overview

This project analyzes **BMW global sales data from 2018 to 2025** using PostgreSQL. The goal is to extract meaningful business insights related to sales performance, regional trends, electric vehicle (EV) adoption, and external economic factors.

The project demonstrates strong SQL skills including **data aggregation, window functions, trend analysis, and business problem solving**.

---

## Dataset Description

The dataset contains the following columns:

* `Year` – Sales year
* `Month` – Sales month
* `Region` – Geographic region
* `Model` – BMW car model
* `Units_Sold` – Number of units sold
* `Avg_Price_EUR` – Average selling price
* `Revenue_EUR` – Total revenue
* `BEV_Share` – Electric vehicle share
* `Premium_Share` – Premium segment share
* `GDP_Growth` – Economic growth indicator
* `Fuel_Price_Index` – Fuel price trend

---

## Objectives

* Analyze **sales performance across regions and years**
* Identify **top-performing and declining models**
* Study **electric vehicle adoption trends**
* Evaluate **impact of fuel prices and GDP growth**
* Build **forecasting and trend analysis using SQL**

## Key SQL Analysis Performed

### Sales & Revenue Analysis

* Total revenue per region and year
* Monthly revenue trends
* Year-over-Year (YoY) revenue growth

### Product Performance

* Top-selling models by region
* Fastest growing BMW models
* Models with declining sales trends

### Electric Vehicle (EV) Insights

* BEV share trend over years
* Region with highest EV adoption
* EV share vs revenue growth analysis

### Advanced Analytics

* Month-over-Month (MoM) growth
* Cumulative sales analysis
* Moving average forecasting
* Seasonal pattern detection

---

## Key Insights

* **Revenue Growth:** BMW shows fluctuating but overall upward revenue trends across years.
* **Regional Performance:** Certain regions dominate revenue contribution consistently.
* **EV Adoption:** BEV share has steadily increased, indicating a shift toward electric vehicles.
* **Fuel Sensitivity:** Some regions show strong dependency on fuel prices affecting demand.
* **Declining Models:** Traditional models show declining trends compared to EV models.

---

## Business Recommendations

* Invest more in **electric vehicle segment** due to rising adoption
* Focus on **high-growth regions** for expansion
* Re-evaluate or redesign **declining models**
* Use **fuel price and GDP indicators** for demand forecasting
